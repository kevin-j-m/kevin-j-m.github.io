Gnarly Endings

  • Oct 15, 2021
  • #career
  • One minute read | 91 words

Today is my last day at The Gnar Company. I joined the company over five years ago excited to work with a great group of individuals, many of whom I’d worked with before. Though many of the people have changed, and the team has grown, the people that work at The Gnar are still some of the greatest people I’ve had the pleasure to work with.

I’m thankful to every member of the team, past and present, who helped make working at The Gnar Company the special experience that it is.
